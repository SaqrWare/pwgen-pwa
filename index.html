Install as App

PwGen is a Progressive Web App — you can install it on your device for quick access and offline use.

Chrome / Edge (Desktop & Android)
  1. Open PwGen in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon (+) in the address bar, or open the browser menu (⋮)
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
Safari (iPhone & iPad)
  1. Open PwGen in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (﻿↑︎) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
Safari (macOS)
  1. Open PwGen in Safari (macOS Sonoma or later)
  2. Click File in the menu bar
  3. Select "Add to Dock"

All passwords are stored locally in your browser. No data is transmitted.
